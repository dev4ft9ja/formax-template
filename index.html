Introduction

Welcome to the General Guides section of the Formaxion Documentation! This section is designed to help you maximize your experience with the platform by providing clear, step-by-step instructions and comprehensive information across a wide range of topics essential for traders.

The primary purpose of this General Guides section is to provide users with clear, concise, and actionable information about how to navigate the Formaxion platform effectively. Whether you are a novice trader unfamiliar with algorithmic trading concepts or an experienced trader looking to leverage advanced features, this section aims to address your needs. By guiding you through various processes—from setting up your account to creating and refining your trading strategies—we strive to make your experience on Formaxion smooth and rewarding.

Getting Started with Formaxion

Creating an Account

Creating an account on Formaxion is the essential first step to accessing the powerful features of the platform. Follow this step-by-step guide:

  1. Visit the Formaxion Website: Navigate to the official website of Formaxion link here .
  2. Click on the Signup Button: Find and click the “Sign Up” button prominently displayed on the homepage.
  3. Fill in Required Information:
    • Email Address: Enter a valid email address that you will use for your account.
    • Password: Create a strong password, ensuring it meets security requirements (a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters).
  4. Submit the Registration Form: Click the “Submit” button to proceed with the registration process.
  5. Check Your Email: After submitting your information, check your inbox for a verification email.

Explanation of the Verification Process

Verification is a crucial step in ensuring the security and integrity of your Formaxion account. The process generally involves:

  • Email Verification: Open the email received from Formaxion and to see the verification code. This action confirms you own the registered email address.
  • Benefits of Verification: Verifying your account protects against unauthorized access and ensures you receive important account-related communications.
  • Complete Verification: Successfully providing the verification code will activate your account, allowing complete access to the Formaxion platform.

Navigating the Dashboard

Once you've logged into your Formaxion account, you will be directed to the dashboard, the central hub of your trading activities. Here is an overview of the key components:

  • Dashboard Components:
    • Menu Options: Access tabs such as 'Home', 'Create Strategy', 'My Profile', 'Community', 'Watchlist', 'Drafts'. Each tab will guide you to different functionalities.
    • Profile Settings: Click on your profile icon located in the top-right corner to update personal information, manage your wallet, logout and access out virtual support.

Account Recovery

Account recovery is a crucial process designed to help users regain access to their Formaxion account in the event they forget their password or encounter other access issues. This guide provides step-by-step instructions for recovering your account securely and efficiently.

  1. Understanding Account Recovery

    Account recovery ensures that users can reclaim access to their accounts while maintaining security. It typically involves verifying your identity using the email address linked to your account.

  2. Steps for Recovering Your Account
    1. Navigate to the Login Page

      Go to the Formaxion login page where you usually enter your credentials. If you’re on the homepage, look for the “Login” button and click on it.

    2. Click on "Forgot Password?"

      On the login form, you will find a link or button labeled "Forgot Password?" Click this link to initiate the recovery process.

    3. Enter Your Registered Email Address

      A prompt will appear asking you to enter the email address associated with your Formaxion account. Make sure to double-check for any typos, as the recovery process will use this email to send instructions.

      • Example: youremail@example.com
    4. Check Your Email Inbox

      After submitting your email address, check your inbox for an email from Formaxion. This email will contain a code to reset your password. Depending on your email service, make sure to check the spam or junk folder if you don’t see it in your main inbox.

    5. Provide The Password Reset Code

      Open the email and locate the code to reset your password. The code is often time-sensitive, so it’s best to act quickly.

    6. Set a New Password

      On the password reset page, you will be prompted to enter a new password. Make sure it meets the platform's security requirements, which may include:

      • A minimum length (usually 8 characters or more)
      • At least one uppercase and one lowercase letter
      • At least one number
      • Special characters (e.g., !, @, #, $)

      Re-enter your new password in the designated field to confirm it and ensure there are no typing errors.

    7. Save Changes and Login

      After successfully setting your new password, click the "Save" or "Submit" button. You will then be redirected to the login page where you can enter your email and new password to access your account.

  3. Troubleshooting Recovery Issues

    If you encounter difficulties during the recovery process, consider the following:

    • Email not received: Ensure you entered the correct email address associated with your account and check your spam/junk folders.
    • Code expired: If the password reset code has expired, repeat the steps to initiate the recovery process again.
    • Account still locked: If your account remains inaccessible after changing your password, contact Formaxion's customer support for further assistance.
  4. Security Tips for Account Recovery
    • Choose a strong, unique password that is not easily associated with you.
    • Regularly monitor your account activity for any unauthorized access.

By following these guidelines, you can easily recover your Formaxion account and ensure your trading experience remains uninterrupted. If you have any further questions or require assistance, do not hesitate to reach out to our support team.

Managing Wallet on Formaxion

Managing your wallet on Formaxion is very simple and seemless. This document covers the various features available for funding your wallet, processing withdrawals, and viewing transaction histories.

  1. Funding Your Wallet

    To empower your trading activities, Formaxion offers the ability to fund your wallet with both local (Naira) and foreign currency. Here’s how you can fund your wallet:

    1. Log in to your account using the following link: Formaxion Login.
    2. Complete your profile setup including your country of residence. This step is important as it helps us provide optimal funding options for your location.
    3. Navigate to the profile section, which is identified by your initials (e.g., for John Doe, it would be JD).
    4. Select the Wallet option from the dropdown menu.
    5. Click on the Deposit button and enter the amount you wish to add to your wallet.
    6. Upon completion, a confirmation message will indicate that your wallet has been successfully funded.
  2. Withdrawing from Your Wallet

    Withdrawing funds is just as easy. Follow these steps to make a withdrawal:

    1. Log in to your account using the following link: Formaxion Login.
    2. Ensure your profile setup is complete.
    3. Navigate to the profile section using your initials.
    4. Select Wallet from the dropdown menu.
    5. Click the Withdraw option and enter the amount you wish to withdraw.
    6. Your withdrawal request will be processed by the admin within 24 hours, provided your profile is fully set up.
    7. A notification will confirm that your withdrawal has been successful.
  3. Transaction History

    To keep track of your financial activities, Formaxion provides users with access to their transaction history. You can view:

    • Deposit records showing all funds added to your wallet.
    • Withdrawal records detailing all funds removed from your wallet.
    • Any other relevant financial transactions within your account.

    Your transaction history can be accessed from your wallet section, offering a clear overview of your trading financials.

Overview of Strategies in Formaxion

Formaxion provides a powerful platform for users to create trading strategies with ease. The platform caters to users with varying levels of expertise, offering two distinct modes for strategy creation:

Modes of Strategy Creation
  • No-Code Mode: This drag-and-drop interface simplifies the strategy-building process, allowing users to visually assemble their trading strategies without the need for programming knowledge. Users can select components from a library of tactical blocks and arrange them to create unique strategies.
  • Code Mode: For those with coding skills, Formaxion offers a code mode, which allows traders to write custom strategies using Python. This mode provides greater flexibility and the ability to develop complex algorithms tailored to specific trading goals.
Building and Testing Strategies

Formaxion emphasizes the importance of strategy validation through backtesting. This feature allows users to:

  • Test their strategies against historical market data to assess performance and risk.
  • Identify potential weaknesses and optimize strategies before deploying them in live trading conditions.
  • Understand how various market conditions might impact their strategy's effectiveness.
Drafting Strategies

After successful creation of a strategy, it is initially saved as a draft. The process of managing drafts is straightforward:

  • Click the "Drafts" button on the menu bar to open your drafts for review and editing.
  • Use this time to refine and perfect strategies before final investment.
Investing and Portfolio Management

To move a strategy from drafts to your portfolio, you must first invest a specified amount. Here’s how the process works:

  • After making a successful investment, the strategy transitions smoothly into your portfolio.
  • In your portfolio, you can monitor and manage active strategies while reviewing performance metrics and outcomes.

Engaging with your portfolio enables a better grasp of how each strategy is performing, ultimately guiding your trading decisions.

Community Engagement

Formaxion also fosters a vibrant community spirit, encouraging traders to share their strategies:

  • Once a strategy has been successfully invested, you can share it with the community.
  • Sharing strategies enhances knowledge exchange and collaboration among traders, allowing users to learn from each other's experiences and insights.

No Code

The drag-and-drop mode in Formaxion provides an intuitive and user-friendly way for individuals to create trading strategies without any coding knowledge. This mode allows users to piece together strategies by visually arranging various tactical blocks tailored to their trading goals.

  1. Creating a Strategy on Formaxion Using the Drag-and-Drop Mode
    1. Step-by-Step Guide to Creating a Strategy
      1. Log In to Your Account
        1. Visit the Formaxion login page.
        2. Enter your credentials to access your account. If you don't have an account, you will need to register first.
      2. Start the Strategy Creation Process
        • Once logged in, find and click on the "Create Strategy" button available in the top menu.
      3. Set Up Your Strategy
        1. Enter a Name for Your Strategy:

          Choose a unique name that clearly describes the purpose of the strategy.

        2. Provide a Description:

          Write a succinct description outlining the strategy's objectives and key components to enhance understanding and identification.

      4. Utilize Tactical Blocks

        During strategy creation, users can leverage various tactical blocks. These blocks represent different functions and asset types used in trading strategies, including:

        • Asset Block: Represents specific stocks or assets that you wish to trade based on your strategy.
        • Condition Block (If/Else): Defines the logical conditions that must be met to trigger specific trading actions.
        • Filter Block: Used to filter and select assets based on specific criteria such as performance metrics.
        • Group Block: Combines multiple tactical blocks into a single cohesive unit, allowing for more complex strategies.
      5. Build Your Strategy
        • Drag and drop the desired tactical blocks from the available selection into the workspace.
        • Arrange and connect the blocks in a logical sequence, following the rules and conditions of your trading strategy.
      6. Backtest Your Strategy

        To evaluate the effectiveness of your strategy, initiate a backtest using historical data:

        • Click the "Backtest" button to analyze how your strategy would have performed in the past.
        • Review the backtest results, identify areas for improvement, and make necessary adjustments to optimize your strategy's performance.
      7. Save Your Strategy
        • After finalizing your strategy, click on the "Save" button to retain your work.
        • Your strategy will initially be saved as a Draft. Once you make an investment based on this strategy, it will be moved to your Portfolio for ongoing management.
    2. AI Co-Pilot Assistance

      The drag-and-drop mode is enhanced by an AI co-pilot that offers additional support. Users can prompt the AI to:

      • Create: Generate new sequences of tactical blocks.
      • Refine: Improve existing strategy blocks.
      • Fill-Up: Complete the next logical block in the strategy.
      • Replace: Substitute sections within the strategy with new content.

FAQ

Formaxion Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs).

What is Formaxion?
Formaxion is an algorithmic trading tool that facilitates both code and no-code trading strategies. Users can create trading strategies using a drag-and-drop interface or code mode, and also backtest their strategies.
Who can use Formaxion?
Formaxion is designed for a wide range of users—from beginners who prefer a no-code approach to experienced traders comfortable with coding their strategies.
How can I get started with Formaxion?

To get started with Formaxion, follow these steps:

  • Create an account on our website.
  • Verify your email address.
  • Log in to your account.
  • Start creating your own strategies!
Is there a cost associated with using Formaxion?
The basic features of Formaxion are available for free, but premium features may require a subscription or fee.
How do I create an account on Formaxion?
To create an account, visit our sign-up page and provide a valid email address. A verification email will be sent to you.
What should I do if I can't log in?

If you experience login issues:

  • Ensure your account is created and verified.
  • If you've forgotten your password, use the “forgot password” option to reset it.
How do I create a trading strategy?

You can create a trading strategy using either:

  • Drag-and-Drop Mode: Use the visual interface to assemble your strategy by dragging components into workspace.
  • Code Mode: Write Python code for advanced strategies.
Can I share my strategies?
Yes, you can share your strategies with the communities.
How do I backtest my strategy?
After creating your strategy, you can backtest it by clicking the "Backtest" button available in the workspace.
How can I fund my wallet?

To fund your wallet:

  • Log into your account.
  • Click on the profile section and select 'wallet.'
  • Click on 'Deposit' and enter the amount you wish to fund.
How do I withdraw funds from my wallet?

To withdraw funds:

  • Log into your account.
  • Go to the profile section and select 'wallet.'
  • Click on 'Withdraw' and enter the withdrawal amount.
How do I backtest my strategy?
After creating your strategy, you can backtest it by clicking the "Backtest" button available in the workspace.
How can I contact Formaxion support?

At Formaxion we have human and virtual support. Go to the support page, start a chat with the virtual support. To switch to a human support, you can request the assistance to transfer the chats to a human support.

You can also contact us via the following channels:

  • Email: info@formaxation.com
  • WhatsApp: +2348138462394
  • Hours: Mon to Fri – 9 AM to 5 PM WAT
Can I suggest new features?
Absolutely! If you have suggestions for features you would like to see in Formaxion, you can submit them through our suggestion form available in the support section of the application.
